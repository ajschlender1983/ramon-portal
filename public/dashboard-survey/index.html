A question, from Adam

Which voice should the portal use when it looks back at you?

The portal is adding a new surface at the top of each user's page — a small summary of sessions, notes, and how their reading has changed over time. Before I build it, I want to make sure it lands the way the portal lands. Five voices below. Read each. Tell me which one your body recognizes. Takes about 5 minutes.

What this surface will never do
  • It will name. It will never grade.
  • Absence is presence. Never a deficit.
  • Specificity comes from quoting you. Not from inferring about you.
  • It opens. It never concludes.
